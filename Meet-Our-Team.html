Meet our team

We are a steadily growing team comprised of over 100 individuals, each one big on character but not on ego. We see ourselves as much more than just a company, but rather a close-knit group of talented, kind, and like-minded individuals who enjoy working together to create amazing things. We value passion, hard work, and flexibility in our team where everyone has the ability to voice their opinion and have an impact on our direction as a company.

Mastan Sab
