Our Story


Mr. Mastan Sab founded eTranBiz Global Technologies in 2011, establishing its headquarters in Bangalore, India.

Since its inception, eTranBiz has predominantly focused on Oracle Products and Technologies addressing complex challenges for our clients.

By 2015, thirty full-time personnel were added across three sites in India: Hyderabad, Bangalore, and Mumbai.

By the end of 2017, we had serviced over twenty banking clients, the vast majority of whom continue to utilise our services.

During the COVID period from 2020 to 2021, we encountered several obstacles such as a lack of resources, delayed payments from clients, sudden loss of multiple key accounts, and an initial difficulty with the transition to remote working, but by the beginning of 2022, we had recovered and are performing even better than before.

We are on the verge of global expansion, with the Middle East, the United Kingdom, Australia, and the United States as our primary priorities.
